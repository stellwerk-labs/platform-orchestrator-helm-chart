1. Get the status of the PostgreSQL cluster:
  kubectl get cluster platform-orchestrator-cnpg-databases -n my-namespace

2. Wait for the cluster to be ready:
  kubectl wait --for=condition=ready pod -l postgresql.cnpg.io/cluster=platform-orchestrator-cnpg-databases -n my-namespace --timeout=300s

3. Connect to the databases (from pods in the same namespace):

   Control Plane DB:
   postgresql://controlplane_user:PASSWORD@platform-orchestrator-cnpg-databases:5432/orchestrator-controlplane

   Data Plane DB:
   postgresql://dataplane_user:PASSWORD@platform-orchestrator-cnpg-databases:5432/orchestrator-dataplane

   IAM DB:
   postgresql://iam_user:PASSWORD@platform-orchestrator-cnpg-databases:5432/orchestrator-iam

4. You can get a database password from a corresponding secret, e.g.:
   kubectl get secret controlplane-db-secret -n platform-orchestrator -o jsonpath='{.data.password}' | base64 --decode
